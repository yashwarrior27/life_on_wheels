About Us - Your home on the go!

Entrepreneur Manas Goel and his mother were always taking travel ventures by road. The only hold-up was the constant discomfort, restlessness, and inconvenience that came along with the long hours of travel. It was certainly no picnic having to stop every few hours for his mom to take a stretch to relieve her back issues, either.

Manas and his mom realized there certainly had to be a better way. One where they could kick back, relax, maybe watch some TV, and eat some food during their inter-city travels. One where they could travel in luxury, with all the amenities one needs to turn their on-road travel experiences from drag to fab. This led to a lightbulb moment for Manas’ mom–why not take your home and all your requisites with you on the go?

Inspired by this notion, Vacation on Wheels was born. This luxury car rental service hopes to provide customers with a smooth and cosy “at home” experience during their travels. How? By combining top-notch vehicles with unparalleled customer service, so every customer gets the VIP treatment they deserve.

Vacation on Wheels provides a timeless experience in the lap of comfort and luxury. It also eases the woes of the handicapped and senior citizens so they can commute easily from one city to another, hindrance-free. And, if you’re a businessman, best believe you’ve met your travel match–work on the road, sleep, whatever you need, you’ll find it with Vacation on Wheels. These caravan rentals are also great for families and parties too! Get ready to VOW your guests!
