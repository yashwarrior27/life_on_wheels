Amenities -You’re in excellent hands

At Vacation on Wheels, we believe it's important to get away from it all and still have everything you need. That’s why our caravans come fully equipped with state-of-the-art amenities to make your travel experience delightful.

Our caravans include

1 Queen Size Bed (2 Person)
1 Microwave
1 Shower Area
1 Washbasin
1 Medium Size Refrigerator
2 LCD TVs
Several Cabinets
1 Recliner Sofa
1 Sofa-cum-bed
1 Dishwasher
1 Portable Speaker
8-10 people Max

All our caravans are wheelchair accessible and can accommodate up to 10 people. The caravans are professionally cleaned and fully maintained, meaning that you can travel with peace of mind. Our service comes with one driver and one helper to provide you with the best in class customer service at all times. We also provide pickup and drop-off services to all clients within our wide range of locations throughout the country.
